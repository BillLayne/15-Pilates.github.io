THE 15 TO FIT METHOD™

The 15 to Fit Method™ was developed to provide a simple, but effective way to improve overall health through a Pilates inspired wellness program.

15 MINUTES OF ENERGY

Whole body movement exercises that increase heart rate and improve cardiorespiratory fitness.

15 MINUTES OF TONE

Targeted exercises to improve overall strength and create a lean, toned appearance.

15 MINUTES OF MOBILITY

Dynamic, multiplanar movements that safely increase mobility of the entire body.

More Effective

Our split carriage design allows for more total body exercises than your average Pilates session.

More Dynamic

Involves more rotational movement and helps release hip flexors as you strengthen abdominals.

Simply Better

A complete workout in 50 minutes with a few minutes of warm-up and cool-down.
